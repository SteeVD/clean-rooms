THE CLEAN ROOMS

Verumo Manufacturing Site 7 produced sterile medical components until the lower-level complex was sealed and the plant decommissioned. The vaulted production halls are dark now — frost has taken the cold-storage wings, growth has reclaimed others. Descend through each level to the emergency exit, following the colored floor lines (blue near the surface, red in the depths) and collecting the keycards needed to unlock it. Things the cleanup contractors never finished removing are still active down here — and something far larger still circles overhead. Kill your flashlight, stay low, and keep out of their path.
